Servus, I'm Gibran!

I’m currently an undergraduate student majoring in business administration at the Faculty of Economics and Management Sciences (KENMS) of International Islamic University Malaysia (IIUM) .

I was born in Jakarta, Indonesia, as the second son of three siblings. Raised in a modest family who taught me to work hard and appreciate the value of money from an early age, I then pursue my interest in finance industry since I was still in high school by learning about stock market and being involved in stock trading.

Previously, I graduated from Insan Cendekia Boarding School (secondary school) with full scholarship from the Ministry of Religious Affairs of the Republic of Indonesia.

Realizing that many of the conventional financial transactions are not aligned with the Shari’ah principles, I decided to further my study in finance that is compliant with Islamic teachings. In attaining my life purpose I believe that focusing more on the present day to do things right is more important than thinking too far about the future.
